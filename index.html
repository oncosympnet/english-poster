Institute and Outpatients Clinic of Medical Psychology

Survey: Symptoms in everyday life

Study for cancer patients

In a scientific study, we are investigating how the symptoms of cancer patients influence each other in everyday life over a longer period of time. To this end, we are conducting a series of short surveys on the symptoms experienced.

Participation:
  • 18 years of age or older
  • Cancer/recurrence diagnosis
  • in German or English
Procedure:
  • Short symptom survey every 3 days for 6 months (approx. 2–3 min)
  • Every 3 months, survey over 12 months (approx. 20–25 min)
Compensation: €120
